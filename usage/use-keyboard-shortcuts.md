# Keyboard shortcuts

Open the **menu** > '**Search**' > '**Documents**' and click the **keyboard icon** at the bottom of the menu:

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/usage/use-keyboard-shortcuts/01-search-documents-page-list-layout-where.png" alt="Screenshot of Datashare&#x27;s search documents page in List layout where the &#x27;Keyboard&#x27; icon at the bottom of the left menu is highlighted"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

It opens a window with the **shortcuts for your OS** (Mac, Windows, Linux):

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/usage/use-keyboard-shortcuts/02-search-documents-page-list-layout-where.png" alt="Screenshot of Datashare&#x27;s search documents page in List layout where the &#x27;Keyboard&#x27; icon at the bottom of the left menu is hovered and the &#x27;Keyboard shortcuts&#x27; popover is highlighted"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click on '**See all shortcuts**' to reach the full page view:

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/usage/use-keyboard-shortcuts/03-keyboard-shortcuts-page.png" alt="Screenshot of Datashare&#x27;s keyboard shortcuts page"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
